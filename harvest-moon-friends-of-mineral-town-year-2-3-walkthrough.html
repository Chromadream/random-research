0. Prerequisites — End of Year 1 Target

⚠️ Before starting Year 2, you should aim to have:
  • 1st House Extension built (3,000G + 200 Lumber)
  • Kitchen purchased via TV Shopping (4,010G) plus Refrigerator + Cabinet
  • Several cooking utensils (Oven, Knife, Frying Pan, Pot minimum)
  • Chicken Coop upgraded (5,000G + 420 Lumber — 8 chicken capacity)
  • Barn upgraded (6,800G + 500 Lumber — 16 livestock capacity)
  • 6-8 chickens laying daily
  • 3-5 cows producing milk
  • 1-2 sheep producing wool
  • All tools at Silver level or higher (Watering Can at Gold recommended)
  • Large Rucksack purchased (5,000G)
  • Basket purchased (5,000G)
  • Bachelorette at Green-Yellow heart or higher
  • Cliff invited to the grape harvest job (so he stays in town!)
  • Horse matured and well cared for
  • Dog matured; Dog Ball purchased; Frisbee training begun
  • At least 3 Adamantite saved from Winter Lake Mine for Maker Machines
  • Some Orichalc saved for jewelry gifts
  • At least 20 recipes discovered
💰 Recommended Bank Balance entering Year 2: 30,000G – 50,000G. If you followed the Year 1 crop strategy (Turnips → Pineapples → Sweet Potatoes) plus Horse Race Broach exploits, you should be in great shape. There is NO Hothouse in FoMT — don't save up lumber for one!
🌱 YEAR 2

1. Year 2 Spring — Expanding the Farm

🎯 Year 2 Spring Goals: 2nd House Extension (10,000G + 700 Lumber) + Big Bed · Purchase Maker Machines (Mayo, Cheese, Yarn) · Raise bachelorette to Red heart · Catch King Fish · Buy 3+ more cows/sheep · Upgrade tools to Mystrile

🌱 Season Overview

Year 2 Spring is when you transition from survival to thriving. With infrastructure in place and upgraded tools, you can manage far more crops. The season's focus is on getting the 2nd House Extension + Big Bed for marriage, upgrading tools to Mystrile, and pushing your chosen bachelorette to Red heart.

Key Events & Strategy

DayEvent / FocusStrategy
Day 1New Year's FestivalFestival day; no farm work. Attend at Rose Square (6 PM). Receive rice cakes — great gifts!
Day 2–7Spring Planting & ExpansionsBuy Cucumbers (bulk) from Supermarket; Cabbages from Won. Plant grass for growing herd. Begin serious lumber chopping for the 2nd House Extension. Craft Jewelry at Saibara (1,000G + Orichalc) — wrap and give to your bachelorette for massive affection!
Day 3Kai ArrivesKai returns for the summer. If pursuing Popuri, this is your rival! He sells food at the Seaside Lodge.
Day 4Bold's BirthdayGive Bold (purple sprite) Flour or Purple Grass.
Day 8Goddess' BirthdayGive the Harvest Goddess a Pineapple or Strawberry if you can reach her.
Day 9–13Maker Machines & CowsBuy the Cheese Maker from Saibara (20,000G + Adamantite). Convert Milk to Cheese for massive profit. Buy more cows — target 8-10.
Day 14Spring ThanksgivingBake Cookies (Flour + Butter + Egg / Rolling Pin + Oven + Sugar). Give to ALL five girls for affection boosts. All girls love cookies on this day — even Karen!
Day 18Spring Horse Race YOUR HORSE!Your horse is mature! Race your own horse and win for a Power Berry. Bet on yourself for massive medals. Buy Broaches (18 medals → 2,000G profit each) + Power Berry (900 medals) + Jewel of Truth (1,000 medals).
Day 19–21Expansion PushBuild the 2nd House Extension (10,000G + 700 Lumber). Also buy the Mayo Maker and Yarn Maker from Saibara if you have Adamantite.
Day 22Cooking FestivalYear 2 theme: Sweets. Enter Chocolate Cake or Cheese Cake. With multiple ingredients added, you should win easily.
Day 23–30Final Marriage PrepOrder the Big Bed from TV Shopping (10,010G — appears after 2nd House Extension + buying all prior items). Purchase remaining kitchen utensils. Give daily wrapped gifts. Your bachelorette should be Orange-Red by month's end.
💎 Jewelry Strategy: Craft Jewelry (Bracelet, Necklace, Earrings, or Broach) at Saibara for 1,000G + 1 Orichalc. Wrap it (100G at Supermarket). Give to ANY bachelorette — all five LOVE Accessories (500 AP). This is the most universally powerful gift in the game. Save your Winter Mine Orichalc specifically for this!

End-of-Spring Checklist

  • 2nd House Extension complete; Big Bed ordered
  • All three Maker Machines purchased (Mayo, Cheese, Yarn)
  • Bachelorette at Red heart ❤️
  • 8-10 cows; 2-4 sheep
  • Won Horse Race (Power Berry obtained)
  • All tools at Gold-Mystrile level
  • All four heart events witnessed
  • Most TV Shopping items purchased

2. Year 2 Summer — Marriage Season

🎯 Year 2 Summer Goals: GET MARRIED! · Win Chicken Sumo Festival · Win Cow Festival · Catch Squid (King Fish) · Max out chicken flock (8) · Fill barn with cows + sheep · Begin deep mining in Spring Mine

☀️ Season Overview

This is the season of love! By now you should have Red heart, the Big Bed, the 2nd House Extension, and all four heart events viewed. The Blue Feather should be at the Supermarket (1,000G). Summer Fireworks (Day 24) is the most romantic proposal backdrop — or propose anytime once conditions are met.

Marriage Checklist

RequirementHow to Fulfill
❤️ Red Heart (60,000-65,535 AP)Give wrapped favorite gifts daily. Talk at festivals. Birthday gift (5×!). Use Jewelry.
🏠 2nd House Extension10,000G + 700 Lumber from Gotz. Provides separate bedroom.
🛏️ Big Bed10,010G from TV Shopping. Only appears after 2nd House Extension + all prior items bought.
💙 Blue Feather1,000G at Supermarket. Appears when any girl is Orange heart+. Don't buy from Won (30,000G rip-off)!
💕 All Four Heart EventsBlack, Purple, Blue (need Large Rucksack + empty item slot), Yellow — in order.
💍 ProposalEquip Blue Feather and present to Red-heart girl. Wedding ~7 days later at Church.

📋 Post-Marriage Routine: Talk to your wife every morning. Give her a liked/loved gift daily (even after marriage). Your wife's heart contributes to your farm completion. She may occasionally cook breakfast for you, restoring stamina. If you married Elli or Mary, they continue working part-time.

3. Year 2 Fall — Settling Down

🎯 Year 2 Fall Goals: Win Sheep Festival · Catch remaining King Fish · Upgrade all tools to Mystrile · Maximize Sweet Potato profits · Full barn (16 animals) · Deep-mine Spring Mine (floor 100+ for Power Berry, Goddess Jewels) · Discover 50+ recipes

🍂 Season Overview

Fall Year 2 is the wealth-building season. Sweet Potatoes in a massive field mean enormous daily income. You should be swimming in gold by mid-season. If you haven't married yet, do it early in Fall. Your wife should begin showing signs of pregnancy toward Winter.

Key Events & Strategy

Day 3Music FestivalGo to the Church at 6 PM. Play ocarina with the girls.

Day 13Full Moon FestivalGo to Mother's Hill peak at 6 PM. Your wife (or highest-heart girl) waits for you.

Day 15Karen's BirthdayGive wrapped Pizza, French Fries, or Wine. Massive affection if befriending her.
Day 18Fall Horse RaceRace again! More medals, more Broaches, more profit. Buy remaining prizes.
Day 21Sheep Festival WIN!Enter an unshorn sheep with most hearts. Win for Golden Wool.

🐑 Sheep Strategy: Enter an unshorn sheep — shorn sheep cannot compete. Wool regrows in 7 days. Yarn (via Yarn Maker) is the most profitable animal product: Yarn (X) sells for 4,000G!

4. Year 2 Winter — Baby & Deep Mining

🎯 Year 2 Winter Goals: Baby born (or pregnancy confirmed) · Win Frisbee Contest training · Deep-mine Spring Mine (floors 100+) for Power Berry + Goddess Jewels · Deep-mine Lake Mine for Kappa Jewels + Cursed Tools · Collect all Power Berries · 100+ recipes discovered · Max tools to Mystrile

❄️ Season Overview

No outdoor crops means you focus on mining, fishing, socializing, cooking, and preparation. The Lake Mine and Spring Mine become your primary activities. If your wife got pregnant in Fall, the baby will arrive around Spring-Summer Year 3.

🍂 YEAR 3

5. Year 3 Spring — Cursed Tools & Completion

🎯 Year 3 Spring Goals: Bless all Cursed Tools · Mine Mythic Stones · Deep-mine Spring Mine floor 255 for Teleport Stone · Baby crawling · Collect remaining Goddess/Kappa Jewels · 108 recipes · Max animal hearts

🌱 Season Overview

Year 3 is about completion. You now have the infrastructure and wealth to tackle the game's deepest content. Focus on blessing cursed tools, reaching floor 255 of the Spring Mine for the Teleport Stone, and maxing out all completion metrics.

Key Events & Strategy

DayEvent / FocusStrategy
Day 1New Year's FestivalNow a family man — attend with your wife. Reflect on your journey.
Day 2–7Spring Planting + Uncurse ToolsPlant Cucumbers + Cabbages. Use Confessional to uncurse Hoe and Watering Can (10 blessings each, 1,000G each). Use the Inn water trick for Axe/Fishing Rod (use 255 times — order free water to restore stamina).
Day 8–13Spring Mine Floor 255 RunDeep-mine to floor 255. Bring food, Black Grass, and save often. The Teleport Stone appears in rocks on floor 255 (Year 3+ only!) Must have empty tool slot! Also grab the Fried Potato recipe from the soil.
Day 14Spring ThanksgivingGive your wife cookies. Give chocolate cookies to all girls for friendship.
Day 18Spring Horse RaceVeteran racer by now. Win medals; buy remaining prizes.
Day 22Cooking FestivalYear 3 theme: Bread. Enter a loaded Sandwich for easy victory.

🔮 Teleport Stone: Found on Spring Mine Floor 255 (rocks), Year 3+. Must have empty tool slot! Warps to any outdoor location instantly. This is a game-changer — no more walking across the map. Required for 100% completion.

8. Year 3 Winter — Teleport Stone & Endgame

🎯 Year 3 Winter Goals: Finish all King Fish · Complete all Power Berries · All 108 recipes · All collectibles (Goddess/Kappa/Truth Jewels) · Max friendship with all villagers · Prepare for Year 4+ endgame

❄️ Season Overview

In FoMT, the game NEVER ends! Unlike BTN's evaluation on Winter 30, you can keep playing forever. This season is about wrapping up anything outstanding and preparing for the long-term endgame goals (Town Cottage, Harvest Goddess marriage, Year 4 rival weddings).

Endgame Goals

GoalHow to Achieve
Town CottageSave 100,000,000G + 999 Lumber. The ultimate wealth display. Go to Gotz to build it.
Marry Harvest GoddessComplete fishing list, shipping list, all 9 Goddess Jewels, all mine items, Big Bed, Year 5+, Carter confessional.
Mountain Cottage50th wedding anniversary gift from your wife. Contains a Goddess Jewel.
Seaside CottageConnect with Harvest Moon: A Wonderful Life (GCN) via link cable. Earn all 42 Link Stars.
All Mythic ToolsBless all Cursed Tools → mine Mythic Stones → pay 50,000G each at Saibara.

♾️ FoMT Never Ends: Unlike Back to Nature, there is NO Mayor evaluation and NO game over. The year counter stops at 200 but you can play forever. This is what makes FoMT the definitive version for completionists. Take your time and enjoy every season!

10. Cursed & Blessed Tools — Deep Dive

Finding Cursed Tools

All 6 regular tools must be at Mystrile level first. Then, in the Lake Mine (Winter), dig in the soil (not rocks) on specific floors. You need an empty tool slot. Once equipped, a cursed tool CANNOT be removed without Carter's help at Confessional (1,000G).

ToolFloorHow to BlessCursed/Blessed Effect
Fishing Rod29Use 255 timesCatch King Fish, Pirate Treasure (10,000G), Fish Fossils (5,000G)
Hoe39Carter bless 10× (1,000G each)Till 12×3 area
Axe49Use 255 timesChop ALL stumps/branches on current screen
Hammer59Equip 10 days straightSmash boulders and large boulders in 1 hit
Watering Can69Carter bless 10× (1,000G each)Water 6×13 area
Sickle79Equip 10 days straightCut 9×9 grass area
💡 Uncursing Tips: For Fishing Rod and Axe (use 255 times), go to the Inn and order free water repeatedly to restore stamina. For Hammer and Sickle (equip 10 days straight), do this in Winter when no crops need tending — hire Sprites for animals. For Hoe and Watering Can (Carter bless 10 times), go to Confessional every Monday/Wednesday/rainy day (1-4 PM), 1,000G per blessing.

Mythic Tools

After blessing ALL 6 tools, Mythic Stones appear in the Spring Mine (floor 60+ and Goddess Jewel floors). Bring one Mythic Stone + 50,000G to Saibara per tool:

Mythic ToolEffect
Mythic HoeTill 18×5 area
Mythic AxeSame as Blessed, 50% less stamina
Mythic HammerSmash ALL stones on current screen
Mythic Watering CanWater 12×21 area
Mythic SickleCut 15×15 grass area
Mythic Fishing RodBetter chance of catching treasures
