1. Game Overview & Key Differences from Back to Nature

Harvest Moon: Friends of Mineral Town (GBA, 2003) is a remake of the PlayStation classic Harvest Moon: Back to Nature. While it carries over the beloved characters, setting, and core gameplay of Mineral Village, it introduces significant improvements and new features that make it arguably the definitive version of the game.

♾️ No Time Limit: Unlike BTN's 3-year evaluation, FoMT never ends. You can play indefinitely — the year counter even stops at 200. There is no "game over" evaluation, so you can farm at your own pace forever.

Core Gameplay Loop

Each day (6:00 AM – 5:50 AM), you manage: water crops → tend animals → harvest crops → forage/mine/fish → socialize → sleep. Time pauses inside buildings and mines but flows freely outside. The year has four 30-day seasons with season-specific crops, foraging items, and festivals. Unlike BTN, you can save at any time from the diary menu.

3. Year 1 Walkthrough — The Critical First Year

Your Year 1 Goal: Get the 1st House Extension + Kitchen, upgrade Chicken Coop and Barn at least once, accumulate 4-8 chickens + 2-3 cows + 1-2 sheep, have your bachelorette at Green-Yellow heart, all tools at Silver+, and build up savings for Year 2 expansions.

🌱 Spring (Year 1)

  • Day 1: Festival day. No farm work possible. The game starts on Spring 2.
  • Day 2: Clear your field (axe + hammer). Forage Blue Grass (near hot spring), Bamboo Shoots (near Mother's Hill lake), and flowers. Ship everything. Play the Harvest Goddess TV game (Left channel) on Days 2–5 — win 10-14 times in a row for Relaxation Tea Leaves (1,000G each!). Buy Turnip seeds with your starting 500G.
  • Day 3: Visit the Poultry Farm — buy 1 Chicken (1,500G) + 100 Chicken Feed. Visit Zack at Mineral Beach (11 AM–5 PM, empty tool slot) to get your free Fishing Rod.
  • Day 4 (Wednesday): Visit Barley at Yodel Ranch to receive your free pony (Wednesdays only!). While you're there, go to the Church between 1–4 PM for Confessional — confess to "Overworking the HS" or "I was cruel to my animals."
  • Day 5–7: Harvest Turnips, reinvest in Cucumbers and Potatoes. Buy the Medium Rucksack (3,000G at Supermarket). Continue clearing field and chopping lumber.
  • Day 8–13: Buy Cabbage seeds from Won (at Zack's house on the beach). Keep gifting your chosen bachelorette daily. Plant grass for future livestock.
  • Day 14: Spring Thanksgiving — give Cookies to girls if you have a Kitchen (unlikely Year 1) or simply talk to them.
  • Day 18: Horse Race — your pony can't race yet. Bet on horses, buy a Power Berry (900 medals) and fill your basket with Broaches (18 medals each, sell for 2,000G!). This is the famous "78K day" money exploit!
  • Day 22: Cooking Festival — Year 1 theme is Juices. If you have no kitchen, you can only watch.
  • By end of Spring: Upgrade Chicken Coop (5,000G + 420 Lumber). Buy 2-3 more chickens. Start upgrading Watering Can and Axe to Copper/Silver.
📺 Harvest Goddess Game: On the first 5 days of Spring, press Left on the TV to play. Guess if numbers are higher or lower. Win 10-14 in a row for Relaxation Tea Leaves (sell 1,000G). You can play 5 times per day — potential 25,000G in the first 4 days!

☀️ Summer (Year 1)

  • Day 1: Beach Day (Frisbee Contest). Your puppy can't compete yet — adult dogs only. Watch and enjoy. The Supermarket is closed — buy Summer seeds on Spring 30!
  • Day 2: Buy Pineapple seeds from Won (1,000G each). Buy Corn seeds from Supermarket. Plant grass.
  • Buy a Cow from Yodel Ranch (5,000G in FoMT — cheaper than BTN's 6,000G!) + Milker (2,000G) + Brush (800G).
  • Day 7: Chicken Sumo Festival — enter your happiest hen. Press A when facing opponent, stop when your chicken is backing up. Win for Golden Eggs!
  • Day 20: Cow Festival — enter your best cow (unlikely to win Year 1).
  • Day 24: Fireworks Display — watch with favorite girl at the beach (6 PM).
  • End of Summer: Upgrade your House — 1st Extension (3,000G + 200 Lumber). Then order the Kitchen from TV Shopping (4,010G). This is critical!

🍂 Fall (Year 1)

  • Buy Sweet Potatoes — plant as many as possible. They're ridiculously profitable.
  • Day 3: Music Festival at the Church (6 PM).
  • Day 9: Harvest Festival — bring an ingredient for the community stew.
  • Day 13: Full Moon Festival — go to Mother's Hill peak at 6 PM. The girl who likes you most will be waiting.
  • Day 14–20: Cliff's Grape Harvest Job! Duke asks for help. You MUST invite Cliff (talk to him at the Inn) or he'll permanently leave at the end of Year 1!
  • Day 21: Sheep Festival. Buy a Sheep (4,000G) + Clippers (1,800G). Enter unshorn sheep.
  • Upgrade Barn (6,800G + 500 Lumber) if possible. Harvest Sweet Potatoes relentlessly.
  • Day 30: Pumpkin Festival — May (6 AM), Stu (8 AM), Popuri (10 AM) visit for candy. Have Chocolate ready!

❄️ Winter (Year 1)

  • No outdoor crops grow. Focus on: mining, fishing, lumber chopping, tool upgrading, friendship building, cooking.
  • Day 2: Mayor Thomas' Request — he asks for a random item. Give it by 7 PM for Golden Lumber (cosmetic only — do NOT use as fence or villagers get angry!).
  • Day 10: Dog Race — train your dog with the Dog Ball (buy from Won). Your dog may still be too young Year 1.
  • Day 14: Winter Thanksgiving — girls who like you bring chocolate/cake. Stay home all day!
  • Day 24: Star Night Festival — ask a girl on the 23rd to have dinner with her family.
  • Day 25: Stocking Festival — need a stocking from Ellen (befriend her + give yarn before Winter 10). Go to sleep 9 PM–12 AM for a gift.
  • Day 30: New Year's Eve — noodles at Rose Square (6 PM) OR sunrise at Mother's Hill (midnight).
  • Mining: The Lake Mine (Winter Mine) is accessible! Mine for rare gems (Diamonds, Pink Diamonds, Alexandrite) and, critically, Adamantite and Orichalc for Maker Machines and jewelry. Also find Cursed Tools on floors 29, 39, 49, 59, 69, 79 (must have all tools at Mystrile first).
  • Both mines have 255 floors — far more than BTN's 10-floor mines!
End of Year 1 Target: Kitchen + Fridge + utensils purchased, 6-8 chickens, 3+ cows, 1-2 sheep, all tools at Silver-Gold, bachelorette at Green-Yellow heart, 3 Adamantite saved, at least 20 recipes discovered. Bank: 30,000G+.

Lumber Gathering Tips

  • Chop branches on your farm daily (1 lumber each)
  • Chop tree stumps on your farm and in the forest (6 lumber each with Copper+ Axe)
  • Buy lumber from Gotz (50G each — expensive but available)
  • Upgrade your Axe to Mystrile ASAP for 1-hit stump chopping
  • Stumps in the forest south of your farm respawn periodically

13. Secrets, Collectibles & Advanced Tips

💎 Collectibles FoMT EXCLUSIVE

  • Goddess Jewels (×9): Found on specific floors in the Spring Mine. Collect all 9 to form the Goddess Gem (recovers stamina when used as a tool). Required to marry the Harvest Goddess.
  • Kappa Jewels (×9): Found on specific floors in the Lake Mine. Collect all 9 to form the Kappa Gem (recovers fatigue when used as a tool).
  • Jewels of Truth (×9): Hidden throughout Mineral Town. Collect all 9 to form the Gem of Truth (displays exact stamina/fatigue values when used). Requires Cabinet to store them. Locations include: doghouse roof, stable trough, 1,000 Horse Race medals, 50,000G from Won, lamp post by Church, Library 2nd floor, TV game in Town Cottage, calendar in Mountain Cottage, Thomas' fridge.
  • Teleport Stone: Found on Floor 255 of the Spring Mine (Year 3+). Must have an empty tool slot. Warps to any outdoor location!

🐝 Honey & Bees

Get honey daily from the beehive in the apple tree on your farm (behind the chicken coop). Honey available year-round — no rare bee event needed like in BTN!

🍳 Spa-Boiled Eggs

Throw regular eggs into the hot spring. They become Spa-Boiled Eggs, selling for 80G (vs. 50G for regular eggs). Great early-game profit boost, and Ann's favorite gift!

📺 4:44 Glitch

Watching TV at exactly 4:44 (AM or PM) causes the game to freeze. This is a glitch from the Japanese version (where 4 is an unlucky number). Save before and avoid!

🌪️ Weather Manipulation

Save before going to bed. Check the next day's weather. If it's a storm/blizzard, reload. The forecast may change! Use this to avoid typhoons destroying your crops.

🏇 Horse Race Exploit

Bring your empty Basket to the Horse Races. Bet on the horse with the LOWEST multiplier (highest chance to win). Exchange all medals for Broaches (18 medals → sell for 2,000G). A basket of 30 Broaches = 60,000G! Do this every Spring 18 and Fall 18.

💒 Confessional Tips

Visit the Church on Monday, Wednesday, or rainy days (1–4 PM). Confessions that matter:

  • "I was cruel to my animals" — raises all animal affection
  • "Townspeople don't like me" — raises all villager friendship
  • "I'm overworking the HS" — raises Harvest Sprite affection
  • "I want to marry the HG" — unlocks Harvest Goddess marriage (must meet all other requirements)

🎯 Golden Lumber Warning

Golden Lumber (from Thomas' Winter Request) NEVER rots but using it as a fence makes ALL villagers hate you (-500 affection with everyone each day it's placed!). Keep it in your cabinet or use it only inside your house.

🏡 Vacation Cottages

  • Mountain Cottage: Gift from your wife on your 50th wedding anniversary
  • Seaside Cottage: Connect FoMT with Harvest Moon: A Wonderful Life (GCN) via GBA-GCN link cable and earn all 42 Link Stars
  • Town Cottage: Build for 100,000,000G + 999 Lumber — the ultimate endgame goal

The 7 Harvest Sprites live behind the Church. Befriending and hiring them is essential for managing a large farm. In FoMT, you can play mini-games with them to increase their skills!

Hiring Sprites

A Sprite must have 3 hearts before you can hire them. Give Flour (50G from Supermarket) — it's their universal loved gift. Once at 3 hearts, talk to them and select "I want your help" to assign them to:

  • Watering: Waters crops (most useful early game)
  • Harvesting: Picks crops and teleports them to shipping bin (very fast!)
  • Animal Care: Feeds and brushes animals (least useful — does a mediocre job)

Sprite Mini-Games FoMT EXCLUSIVE

Playing mini-games with sprites increases their skill level, making them work faster:

  • Watering Game: Memory game — remember which squares got watered. Hardest mini-game.
  • Harvesting Game: Tap A rhythmically to harvest turnips. Don't tap too fast!
  • Animal Husbandry Game: Mostly useless — sprites do a poor job caring for animals anyway.
💡 Sprite Strategy: Befriend sprites with Flour year-round. Hire 3-4 for watering and 3-4 for harvesting during busy seasons. Rotate them to keep them working daily. During Winter, give them gifts to boost friendship for next year. The Tea Party invitation (Spring, random) gives Relaxation Tea Leaves!

17. Multi-Run Mastery Plan

Since FoMT has no time limit, you can theoretically do everything in one file. However, seeing all marriage candidates, rival marriages, and fully experiencing the game benefits from multiple playthroughs:

Run 1: The Learning Run

  • Focus on money accumulation: Turnips → Pineapples → Sweet Potatoes
  • Build all farm expansions
  • Marry your favorite bachelorette by Year 2
  • Collect all 10 Power Berries
  • Save 3 Adamantite for Maker Machines
  • Win Chicken, Cow, and Sheep festivals
  • Keep Cliff in town (grape harvest job!)
  • Collect the Teleport Stone (Year 3+)

Run 2: The Completionist Run

  • Marry a different bachelorette (or the Harvest Goddess!)
  • Catch all 6 King Fish
  • Collect all Goddess Jewels, Kappa Jewels, and Jewels of Truth
  • Get all Cursed → Blessed → Mythic tools
  • Win every festival at least once
  • Discover all 108 recipes
  • Ship every shippable item
  • Build the Town Cottage (100,000,000G!)

Run 3–5: Romance All Bachelorettes

  • Each run, marry a different girl to see all heart events, wedding scenes, and rival marriages
  • Experience baby/child scenarios with each wife
  • Witness different rival weddings by befriending the rival bachelors
